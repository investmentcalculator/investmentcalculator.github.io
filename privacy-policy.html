Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Investment Calculator. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

Investment Calculator is a client-side application. All calculations are performed directly in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your financial data or calculation inputs to our servers.

  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not store your calculation data
  • We do not track your browsing behavior

3. Cookies

We may use cookies for basic functionality such as remembering your preferences. These cookies do not contain any personal or financial information.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and hosting purposes. These services have their own privacy policies and we recommend reviewing them.

5. Data Security

Since all calculations happen in your browser and we don't collect your data, your financial information remains private and secure on your device.

6. Your Rights

As we don't collect personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain full control over your information at all times.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
